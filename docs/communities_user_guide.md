# Communities User Guide

Welcome to BookShare Communities! This guide will help you get started with creating, joining, and managing communities to share books with specific groups.

---

## What are Communities?

Communities are groups where you can share books with specific people who share your interests. Whether it's your book club, university campus, neighborhood, or interest-based group (like Sci-Fi lovers), communities help you organize and share books with the right people.

---

## Getting Started

### Discovering Communities

1. **Navigate to Communities**
   - Click "Communities" in the main navigation menu
   - You'll see the Communities page with two tabs: "Discover" and "My Communities"

2. **Browse Available Communities**
   - **Discover Tab**: Shows all public communities you can join
   - **My Communities Tab**: Shows communities you're already a member of
   - Use the search bar to find communities by name or description

3. **Community Privacy**
   - **Public Communities** üåê: Anyone can join immediately
   - **Private Communities** üîí: Require approval from community admins/owners

## Joining a Community

### Public Communities

1. Find a community you'd like to join in the "Discover" tab
2. Click the **"Join"** button on the community card
3. You're instantly a member! You'll now see the community in "My Communities"

### Private Communities

1. Find a private community (indicated by the üîí badge)
2. Click the **"Request to Join"** button
3. Wait for a community owner or admin to approve your request
4. You'll see a **"Pending"** badge while waiting
5. Once approved, you'll have full access to the community

## Creating a Community

1. **Start Creating**
   - Click the **"Create Community"** button on the Communities page
   - A creation modal will appear

2. **Fill in Details**
   - **Name** (required): Choose a descriptive name for your community
   - **Description** (optional): Explain what your community is about
   - **Location** (optional): Where your community is based (e.g., "San Francisco, CA", "MIT Campus", "Downtown Seattle")
   - **Avatar URL** (optional): Add an image URL to make your community stand out
   - **Privacy Settings**:
     - ‚òê **Make this community private**: Check to require approval for new members
     - ‚òê **Require approval for new members**: Check to manually approve join requests

3. **Create**
   - Click **"Create Community"**
   - You're automatically set as the community owner
   - Your community appears immediately in "My Communities"

## Adding Books to Communities

### When Adding a New Book

1. Navigate to "My Library" ‚Üí "Add Book"
2. Fill in the book details
3. Scroll to the **"Share with Communities"** section
4. Check the communities where you want to share this book
5. Click **"Add Book"**
6. The book is now visible to members of selected communities

### For Existing Books

1. Go to "My Library"
2. Click on a book you own
3. Click the **"Edit"** button
4. In the edit modal, scroll to the **"Communities"** section
5. Check/uncheck communities to add/remove the book
6. Click **"Save Changes"**

**Note**: You can only add books you own to communities. You must be an approved member of the community to share books there.

## Browsing Community Books

### Filter by Community

1. Go to the **"Browse"** page
2. Look for the **"Community"** filter in the sidebar
3. Select from:
   - **"All Books"**: Shows all available books (default)
   - **"My Communities"**: Shows books from all communities you're in

### View Community Books

1. Go to a community detail page
2. Click the **"Books"** tab
3. Browse all books shared in this community
4. Use search and filters to find specific books

## Managing Your Community

*Only available if you're a community owner or admin*

### Accessing Community Settings

1. Navigate to your community detail page
2. Click the **"Settings"** tab
3. You'll see options to edit or delete the community

### Editing Community Details

1. In the Settings tab, find the "Edit Community" section
2. Update the name, description, location, avatar, or privacy settings
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Managing Members

1. Go to your community detail page
2. Click the **"Members"** tab

**As an Owner or Admin, you can:**

- **Approve/Deny Join Requests**: See pending requests at the top
  - Click ‚úì to approve
  - Click ‚úó to deny

- **Promote Members**:
  - Click **"Promote to Admin"** to give admin privileges
  - Admins can manage members and settings (but can't delete the community)

- **Demote Admins**:
  - Click **"Demote to Member"** to remove admin privileges

- **Remove Members**:
  - Click **"Remove"** to remove a member from the community

### Transferring Ownership

*Only available to community owners*

If you need to transfer ownership:

1. Go to **Settings** tab
2. Scroll to **"Transfer Ownership"** section
3. Select a member from the dropdown (only admins and regular members shown)
4. Click **"Transfer Ownership"**
5. Confirm in the dialog
6. The selected member becomes the new owner
7. You become an admin

**Important**: You cannot leave a community while you're the owner. You must either:
- Transfer ownership to someone else first, OR
- Delete the community

### Deleting a Community

*Only available to community owners*

‚ö†Ô∏è **Warning**: This action is permanent and cannot be undone!

1. Go to **Settings** tab
2. Scroll to **"Danger Zone"**
3. Click **"Delete Community"**
4. Type the community name to confirm
5. Click **"Delete"**

When deleted:
- The community and all member associations are removed
- Books remain intact but are no longer associated with the community
- All activity history is deleted

## Tips for Success

### For Community Owners

- **Choose descriptive names**: Make it easy for people to understand what your community is about
- **Add a description**: Explain the purpose, rules, or focus of your community
- **Set clear privacy settings**: Public for open sharing, Private for controlled membership
- **Promote active members to admins**: Share the management workload
- **Keep the activity feed active**: Encourage members to share and borrow books

### For Members

- **Share your best books**: Add books to communities where they'll be appreciated
- **Engage with the community**: Borrow books, leave reviews, and participate
- **Respect community guidelines**: If a community has specific rules, follow them
- **Leave thoughtful reviews**: Help others discover great books

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I share the same book in multiple communities?**
A: Yes! When adding or editing a book, you can select multiple communities.

**Q: Who can see books I share in a community?**
A: Only approved members of that community can see and borrow those books.

**Q: Can I remove a book from a community?**
A: Yes! Edit the book and uncheck the community you want to remove it from.

**Q: What's the difference between owner and admin?**
A: Owners can transfer ownership and delete the community. Admins can manage members and settings but cannot delete or transfer ownership.

**Q: Can I be in multiple communities?**
A: Yes! You can join as many communities as you want.

**Q: How do I find communities near me?**
A: Communities can include a location field (e.g., "Boston, MA" or "UC Berkeley Campus"). Use the search bar on the Communities page to search by location. The search looks through community names, descriptions, and locations to help you find relevant communities in your area.
